The Best Movies Of All Time; According To Keith

My Top 3 Favorite Movies Of All Time.

Halloween 1 & 2

Listen, I know this is the first one and all, and that technically it isn't a single movie so I shouldn't be allowed to list it, but fuck that. Halloween 1 & 2 were suspense masterpieces that drove fear into every kid on Halloween in the 80s. I could rave about the movies for hours.

The Untouchables

Same thing here. Old school movie, great adaptation of a story, strong characters. Sean Connery as an onery old ass Chicago PD beat cop just looking for a reason to believe in people again. UGH. Amazing. Movie.

Hush

This one is much more recent. A thriller more than "proper" horror but the kind of movie that makes you sleep with the lights on for a night or two. Beautifully done!
