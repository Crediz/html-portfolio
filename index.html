Movie Ranking Project

My personal top three favorite movies of all time.

A Walk To Remember

This is probably forever cemented in the position of being my favorite movie of all time. Shane West's character goes from being a random punk to a really good guy and it gives me hope that I won't suck forever.

The Untouchables

Robert DeNiro as Al Capone, Kevin Costner as Elliot Ness, "They get a bat, you get a gun. That's the Chicago way!" do I really even need to explain this one?

Halloween

This is a series, not a movie, but I'm a big fan. Micheal is just pure terror in a realistic(ish) fashion and I love seeing it.
